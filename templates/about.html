About EV Charging Prediction

Our platform helps EV owners predict their vehicle’s driving range based on key factors like:

  • Top Speed (Km/h)
  • Acceleration (Sec)
  • Efficiency (Wh/km)
  • Number of Seats
  • Brand & Power Train
  • Body Style & Segment
  • State of Charge (%)

Using these parameters, we predict how far your EV can go on its current charge with improved accuracy and real-time updates.

Nearby Charging Station Locator

Integrated with OpenChargeMap API, our locator helps users find the nearest EV charging stations based on:

  • Real-time user location
  • Live station availability
  • Navigation assistance

Charging Service Booking

With our smart booking feature, users can:

  • Select city-specific stations
  • Choose charging type (Fast, Slow, DC, AC)
  • Reserve slots to avoid waiting
